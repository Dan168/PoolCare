How It Works

Log. Monitor. Analyse. Improve.

Our software simplifies and streamlines the process of recording pool tests and plant room maintenance, ensuring that your pool operations run smoothly and efficiently – saving you time, labour, and money.



1. Log.

Once your account is set up, you can begin adding pool tests. This can be done as and when needed, with the flexibility to record tests on a daily or weekly basis. Our intuitive interface allows you to easily input test results, including parameters such as chlorine levels, pH levels, alkalinity, and more.

2. Monitor (Notifications)

To keep your management team informed, our software sends automatic email notifications to managers whenever a pool test is completed. These emails include a summary of the test results, highlighting any critical issues that require immediate action. This ensures that your team can quickly address any problems and maintain optimal pool conditions.

3. Analyse

Our main dashboard provides a centralized view of all your pools, offering valuable insights through various graphs and performance metrics. You can monitor uptime and downtime, track maintenance activities, and view historical data trends. This visual representation helps you quickly assess the status of each pool and make data-driven decisions to enhance your operations.

4. Improve!

By using our software, you gain access to a wealth of data that can be used to continuously improve your pool operations. Analyse trends, identify recurring issues, and implement best practices to enhance water quality and reduce downtime. Our software empowers you to take proactive measures and ensure the highest standards of pool maintenance. With our user-friendly software, managing pool tests and plant room maintenance has never been easier. Stay informed, stay efficient, and keep your pools in top condition with our comprehensive solution.
