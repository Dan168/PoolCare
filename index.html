How It Works

Log. Monitor. Analyse. Improve.

Our software simplifies and streamlines the process of recording pool tests and plant room maintenance, ensuring that your pool operations run smoothly and efficiently – saving you time, labour, and money.



1. Log.

Once set up, pool tests can be added at pre-set times throughout the day. Our easy to use, intuitive interface allows users to use the system with little training.

2. Monitor (Notifications)

To keep your team informed, the system automatically emails managers and maintenance staff if an out of permitter test is reported. This ensures your team can quickly address any issues and maintain optimal pool conditions.

3. Analyse

The dashboard provides insights into each pool and its water chemistry, offering valuable insights through various graphs including which pools have the most issues and therefore need the most attention.

4. Improve!

By using the system, you gain a wealth of data that can be used to provide insights and therefore reduce maintenance costs by catching issues before they become too major, thus preventing bigger problems and enhancing pool uptime. This, in turn, can improve member retention by ensuring better pool availability.
